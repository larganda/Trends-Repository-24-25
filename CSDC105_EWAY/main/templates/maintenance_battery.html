Battery Maintenance Guide

Proper battery care is crucial for ensuring your e-bike operates efficiently and for prolonging its lifespan.

Charging Tips:

  • Charge after each ride: Always recharge your battery after a ride, even if you haven't used all the battery power.
  • Avoid overcharging: Don't leave your battery plugged in for extended periods once it's fully charged.
  • Charge in a cool, dry place: Avoid extreme temperatures while charging (either too hot or too cold).

Storage Tips:

  • Store partially charged: If you're not using your bike for a while, store the battery at 50â€“70% charge.
  • Cool, dry storage: Store the battery in a cool, dry location to prevent damage.

Battery Health:

  • Regularly check the battery: Ensure the battery is securely mounted, clean, and shows no signs of wear or corrosion.
  • Monitor performance: If the battery life is significantly reduced, it may need to be replaced.
